- Indian cuisines (regional: North, South, East, West, Northeast)
- Indian dietary patterns (vegetarian majority, religious restrictions, fasting days)
- Indian family structures (nuclear vs joint families)
- Local grocery delivery services (BigBasket, Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, etc.)
- Indian nutrition databases (local ingredients, recipes)
- Pricing for Indian market (different from US pricing)
- Multi-language support (Hindi + regional languages)
- Indian festivals and seasonal cooking
